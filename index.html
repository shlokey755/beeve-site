Welcome to BeeVe Global

Empowering businesses through intelligent connectivity

BeeVe Global is a leading technology design, development, manufacturing and system integration company specialising in IoT, GPS tracking, telematics, artificial intelligence, smart automation and advanced technology solutions.

Our experienced team of engineers, software developers, embedded systems experts and IoT specialists takes every product from concept to deployment — integrating intelligent hardware, embedded firmware, cloud platforms, web and mobile applications, AI-powered analytics and enterprise dashboards.

Our journey began in 2014 with the establishment of The Lioness Solutions Ltd. in Rwanda, Africa, followed by Oasis Food & Beverages Ltd. in 2015 — the foundation on which BeeVe Global has grown into a trusted global technology partner.
